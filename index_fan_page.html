Learn About London in 5 Minutes (or less)



London is one of the most popular destinations in the world, with millions of enthusiastic tourists flocking here each year. And it’s no wonder with its wealth of culture and history. However, no matter how many times you visit, there are sure to be lots of fun ‘did you know’ facts about London that you might have missed.

https://travel.earth/did-you-know-facts-12-fascinating-facts-about-london/


Here are four facts

  1. London has four UNESCO world heritage sites: Tower of London, Maritime Greenwich, Westminster Palace and Kew's Royal Botanic Gardens.
  2. There are more than 22,000 music performances a year across London's 300+ venues including the O2 arena - the world's most popular music venue.
  3. London has more than 800 bookshops and over 325 public libraries including the British Library, which holds the Magna Carta.
  4. The London Underground escalators travel the distance of the circumference of the earth each week!
